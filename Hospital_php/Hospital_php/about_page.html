Infection Prevention Control

IPC was established in 1983 by Dr. Prathap C Reddy, IPC Hospitals revolutionized healthcare in India as the nation's first corporate hospital. Now Asia's leading integrated healthcare provider, IPC encompasses hospitals, pharmacies, clinics, telemedicine, and educational institutions. Pioneering clinical excellence and affordability, IPC has embraced technology, introducing cutting-edge innovations like South East Asia's first Proton Therapy Centre. With a patient-centric ethos, IPC has served over 150 million individuals from 140 countries, emphasizing Tender Loving Care (TLC). Beyond business, IPC promotes preventive healthcare through initiatives like the Billion Hearts Beating Foundation, addressing non-communicable diseases. Socially responsible, IPC supports underprivileged children through initiatives like SACHi and SAHI, while the Total Health Foundation pilots holistic healthcare models. Recognized by the Government of India and honored with stamps, Dr. Prathap C Reddy, IPC's Founder Chairman, was awarded the Padma Vibhushan for his contributions.
